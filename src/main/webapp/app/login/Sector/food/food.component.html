India's diverse climate ensures the availability of all varieties of fresh fruits & vegetables. It ranks second in fruits and vegetable production in the world, after China.

Amongst fruits, the country ranks first in the production of Bananas (26.45%), Mangoes (including mangosteens and guavas) (43.80%)and Papayas (39.30%).
The vast production base offers India tremendous opportunities for export.
